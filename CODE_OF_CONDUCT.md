# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct (v3.0)

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make our community welcoming, safe, and equitable for all.

We are committed to fostering an environment that respects and promotes the
dignity, rights, and contributions of all individuals, regardless of race,
ethnicity, caste, color, age, physical characteristics, neurodiversity,
disability, sex or gender, gender identity or expression, sexual orientation,
language, philosophy or religion, national or social origin, socio-economic
position, level of education, or other status.

Participation in this community is open to everyone who acts in good faith
and in accordance with this Code of Conduct.

---

## Encouraged Behaviors
We strive to create a positive and inclusive environment by:

- Respecting the purpose of the project and the community
- Engaging kindly, honestly, and constructively with others
- Respecting different viewpoints and experiences
- Taking responsibility for our actions and contributions
- Gracefully giving and accepting constructive feedback
- Committing to repairing harm when it occurs
- Acting in ways that support the well-being of the community

---

## Restricted Behaviors
The following behaviors are not acceptable and are violations of this Code
of Conduct:

- Harassment, intimidation, or abusive behavior
- Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory comments
- Discrimination or stereotyping based on identity or background
- Sexualized language or inappropriate behavior
- Sharing private or confidential information without consent
- Encouraging or threatening violence or harm toward any person or group
- Any behavior that threatens the safety or well-being of the community

---

## Other Restrictions
- Impersonating another individual or misleading identity
- Failing to properly credit sources or original authors
- Posting promotional, spam, or commercial content outside project norms
- Irresponsible communication that promotes or links to restricted behaviors

## Enforcement & Addressing Harm
Project maintainers will take appropriate action based on the severity and
impact of the violation. Enforcement actions may include:

1. **Warning**  
   A private written warning with guidance on expected behavior.

2. **Temporary Restrictions**  
   A time-limited restriction on participation or communication.

3. **Temporary Suspension**  
   Temporary removal from community spaces with conditions for return.

4. **Permanent Ban**  
   Permanent removal from the community for severe or repeated violations.

Enforcement actions are carried out privately to protect the safety and
privacy of all parties involved.

---

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies to all community spaces related to this project,
including GitHub repositories, discussions, pull requests, issues, and any
public spaces where an individual is representing the project.
